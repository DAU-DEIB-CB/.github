## üèõÔ∏è About Us

The **Data Analytics Unit (DAU)** is a specialized technical team within the **Digital Enforcement and Intelligence Branch (DEIB)** of the **Competition Bureau Canada**.

### Our Ecosystem

1.  **The Competition Bureau** protects and promotes competition for the benefit of Canadian businesses and consumers.
2.  **The DEIB** leverages data and technology to support enforcement initiatives, gathering intelligence on how companies use technology to compete or harm competition.
3.  **The DAU** develops the code-based tools, automation, and analytical models that power these investigations.

We provide specialized support to case teams using **Machine Learning**, **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**, **Geospatial Analysis**, and **Automated Web Capture**.
